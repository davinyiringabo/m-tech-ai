# Acme Cloud — Account & Security

## Password reset
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot password". A reset
link is sent to your registered email and is valid for 60 minutes. If the email
doesn't arrive within 5 minutes, check your spam folder and confirm you are using
the email address on file. Reset links can only be used once.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA under Settings → Security. We support
authenticator apps (TOTP) and hardware security keys. SMS-based 2FA is not
supported because it is less secure. If you lose your 2FA device, use one of the
backup codes shown when you enabled 2FA.

## Account lockout
After 10 failed login attempts an account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes to
prevent brute-force attacks. Locked accounts unlock automatically; there is no
need to contact support. Repeated lockouts may indicate a compromised password.

## Data export and deletion
You can export all of your data at any time under Settings → Privacy → Export.
Account deletion is permanent and removes all data after a 30-day grace period.
