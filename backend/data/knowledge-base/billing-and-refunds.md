# Acme Cloud — Billing & Refunds

## Billing cycle
Acme Cloud bills monthly on the calendar day you first subscribed. Invoices are
generated at 00:00 UTC and emailed to the account's billing contact. You can view
all past invoices under Settings → Billing → Invoices.

## Refund policy
We offer a full refund within 14 days of any charge if you are not satisfied.
Refunds are issued to the original payment method and take 5–10 business days to
appear. Duplicate or accidental charges are always refunded in full, with no time
limit. To request a refund, email billing@acme.example or open a ticket with your
invoice number.

## Plan changes
Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the
start of the next billing cycle. You will never be charged twice for the same
period when changing plans.

## Failed payments
If a payment fails, we retry three times over six days. Your service stays active
during this grace period. After the third failed attempt the account is paused,
not deleted, and all data is retained for 30 days.
